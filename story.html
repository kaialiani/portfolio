A story without words

This small project had the purpose of starting to learn video editing using Adobe Premiere Pro. We had to create a 1 minute video that tells a story without using any words (neither spoken nor written).

CONCEPT

Considering the short time given to make this, we were planning to go to Christiania in the afternoon and decided to record the video there. We wanted to show the difference in physical sensations before and after smoking cannabis, so we took a normal shot of the nature surroundings and then, after a short clip of the finished joint, I used the first shot with some light/color tweaks to make it look more vibrant and saturated, as if the viewer was high.
