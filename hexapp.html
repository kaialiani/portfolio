#HEX App

CONCEPT

We wanted to develop an application that allows you to capture any color of your surroundings and apply it to t-shirts. The idea was to playfully guide the user through the creation process, without getting them bored or frustrated along the way. My role in the group was to design the visuals of the app and to make the XD prototype.

VISUAL DESIGN

We've used a neutral but friendly font type and kept the general colourscheme as a classic b/w contrast with grey accents. This keeps the focus on the colours of the picture/t-shirt and allows the user to get caught in the playful creativity flow without getting disturbed by visually noisy elements. We've used familiar icons to keep everything as intuitive as possible and recognisable to the user. The familiar visual ques used will help the user move around the interface independently and help them maintain a creative flow.
